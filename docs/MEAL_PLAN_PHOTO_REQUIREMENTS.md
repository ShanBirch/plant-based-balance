## Photo Specifications

### File Format
- Primary: PNG (best quality)
- Alternative: WebP (smaller file size)
- Avoid: JPG (unless existing)

### Dimensions
- Minimum: 1200 x 800 px
- Recommended: 1600 x 1067 px
- Aspect Ratio: 3:2

### Naming Convention
```
{plan_prefix}_{meal_name_snake_case}.png

Prefixes:
- ss_ = Summer Shred
- cb_ = Clean Bulk
- eb_ = Energy Boost
- gr_ = Gut Reset
- qe_ = Quick & Easy
- nat_ = Natalie's plan (existing)
```

/assets/meal-plans/clean-bulk/

## Notes for Photography

### Summer Shred Style
- Bright, fresh, energetic
- Show portion control (smaller plates)
- Emphasize protein and vegetables
- Minimal carbs visible
- Active/fitness vibe

### Clean Bulk Style
- Hearty, abundant portions
- Show full plates/bowls
- Emphasize carbs and protein together
- Masculine energy but not exclusive
- Satisfying, substantial meals

### Energy Boost Style
- Vibrant colors (greens, oranges, yellows)
- Dynamic presentation
- Morning light feel
- Fresh and alive ingredients
- Show adaptogens/superfoods

### Gut Reset Style
- Soft, calming colors
- Gentle, approachable
- Simple presentations
- Nothing too spicy or intense looking
- Healing/nurturing vibe

### Quick & Easy Style
- Clean, minimal setup
- Show time-saving elements
- Kitchen context (counter, simple utensils)
- Casual, realistic presentation
- "I can do this" feel
