The All-or-Nothing Trap

Why perfectionism is the enemy of progress.

"I ate a cookie, so I might as well eat the whole box and start again on Monday." Sound familiar? This all-or-nothing mindset is the number one reason women fail to sustain weight loss.

The 80/20 Rule

Consistency beats intensity every time. Aim to be "on plan" 80% of the time. Save the other 20% for life—birthday cake, a glass of wine, a missed workout.

When you remove the guilt from these "off" moments, they stop spiraling into week-long binges. You enjoyed the treat, and now you simply step back onto the path at your next meal.

Journaling

Taking 5 minutes in the morning to set your intentions can rewire your brain. Instead of focusing on what you *can't* eat, write down how you want to *feel*. "Today I choose energy. Today I choose to nourish myself."
