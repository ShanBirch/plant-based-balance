7-Day Rapid Kickstart

Phasing Out Inflammation & Resetting Metabolic Signals

The Goal

Welcome to Phase 0. Before we enter the full 28-day protocol, we must clear the internal "static." For many plant-based women, we are over-reliant on inflammatory oils (canola, sunflower) and heavy processed soy which can spike cortisol and cause fluid retention.

"This week is about lowering visceral inflammation. If you follow this exactly, most women lose 1.5kg - 3kg of 'inflammatory weight' (water/toxins) in just these 7 days."
The Golden Rules
  • No Seed Oils: Use only extra virgin olive oil, coconut oil, or avocado oil.
  • The Morning Flush: 250ml warm water + half a lemon + 1 tsp ginger. Drink before coffee!
  • Zero Alcohol: For 7 days, we give the liver a total break to process estrogen.
  • The 12-Hour Window: Ensure at least 12 hours between your last meal and your first (e.g., Finish at 7pm, start at 7am).
