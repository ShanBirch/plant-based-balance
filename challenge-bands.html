The Power of Resistance

1

Variable Resistance

Bands get harder as you stretch them, mirroring your muscle's strength curve. This allows for maximum muscle fiber recruitment without the joint strain of heavy weights.

2

Specifically designed to hit those hard-to-reach areas like the backs of the arms (triceps), inner thighs, and side glutes for a total body definition reset.

The constant tension forces your body to adapt and repair, creating a metabolic "afterburn" that keeps you burning calories for hours after your session.
