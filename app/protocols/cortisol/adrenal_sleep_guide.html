The Adrenal Sleep Architecture Guide

"It's not just about 'sleeping more'. It's about 'Sleep Architecture'. This guide teaches you the exact evening routine to lower your body temperature and turn off the 'adrenaline switch' before bed, so you stay asleep until your alarm."

1

The Wind Down Routine

10 Hours Pre-Bed
The Caffeine Cutoff Switch to herbal tea or water. Caffeine stays in your system for up to 10 hours, keeping cortisol artificially high.
3 Hours Pre-Bed
Kitchen Closed Finished your last meal. Digestion raises body temperature; we need it to drop for deep sleep onset.
2 Hours Pre-Bed
The Blue Light Block Laptops closed. Phone on 'Night Shift' mode. If possible, wear blue-light blocking glasses. Adrenaline is triggered by blue light.
1 Hour Pre-Bed
Thermal Dump Take a warm shower or bath. When you step out, your body temperature will rapidly drop, mimicking the natural sleep signal your biology craves.
15 Mins Pre-Bed
The Brain Dump Write down your top 3 'Must Do' items for tomorrow on paper. Get them out of your head so your brain doesn't loop on them all night.

Bedroom Setup Checklist

Temperature Drop
Set thermostat to 65-68°F (18-20°C). Cool rooms promote deeper REM cycles.
Total Blackout
Cover all LEDs (even the TV standby light). Use blackout curtains or a sleep mask.
Phone Quarantine
Charge your phone in the bathroom or hallway, not on the nightstand.
White Noise
Use a fan or sound machine to mask sudden noises that trigger 'light sleeper' wakeups.
