Hydration Hacks

More than just drinking water.

Fatigued? Hungry? Headache? 90% of the time, you're just dehydrated. But chugging plain water can sometimes flush out electrolytes, leaving you feeling *more* thirsty.

Eat Your Water

Cucumbers, watermelon, celery, and oranges are packed with structured water that your cells can easily absorb. Snack on these throughout the day.

Morning Elixir

Start every day with 16oz of warm water with lemon and a pinch of pink Himalayan salt. This adrenal cocktail supports your stress glands and wakes up your digestion.
