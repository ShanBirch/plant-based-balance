Green Goddess Smoothie Bowl

The perfect metabolic starter for your day.

Morning breakfast can be a challenge. You need something fast, but you also need enough protein to kickstart your metabolism and keep blood sugar stable. Enter the Green Goddess Smoothie Bowl.

This isn't your average fruit-heavy smoothie that leaves you hungry in an hour. This is packed with healthy fats and fiber to keep you satiated.

The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 avocado (creamy healthy fats!)
  • 1 handful spinach (you won't taste it, promise)
  • 1 serving vanilla plant-based protein powder
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango or kiwi
  • 1 cup almond milk

Toppings:

  • Granola
  • Coconut flakes
  • Sliced kiwi

Instructions:

  1. Blend all base ingredients until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour into a bowl.
  3. Artfully arrange your toppings (we eat with our eyes first!).
  4. Enjoy efficiently.
